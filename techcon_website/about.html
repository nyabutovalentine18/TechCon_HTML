Our History

From humble beginnings to global impact

[I

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of visionary technologists who believed in the power of collaboration to drive innovation. What started as a small gathering of 200 enthusiasts in a San Francisco conference center has grown into one of the world's most influential technology events.

Over the past 14 years, TechCon has expanded to host events on four continents, featuring thousands of speakers and attracting attendees from over 120 countries. Our journey reflects the exponential growth of the technology sector itself, evolving from a focus on software development to encompass AI, blockchain, quantum computing, and sustainable tech.

TechCon 2010 - Our inaugural event with 200 attendees

TechCon 2015 - Our first international expansion

TechCon 2022 - Record attendance of 15,000+ participants

Each year, we've pushed boundaries, introduced groundbreaking technologies to the mainstream, and created a platform where the next generation of innovators can connect with established industry leaders. Our commitment to fostering an inclusive, forward-thinking community has remained constant throughout our growth.

Our Mission

Driving technological progress for a better future

At TechCon, our mission is to accelerate positive global change through technology. We believe that by bringing together diverse perspectives, we can solve complex challenges and create a more equitable, sustainable, and connected world.

Years of Innovation

Our core principles guide everything we do:

  • Innovation Through Collaboration: We believe the most transformative ideas emerge at the intersection of different disciplines and perspectives.
  • Ethical Technology Development: We champion responsible innovation that considers societal impact and ethical implications.
  • Accessibility and Inclusion: We're committed to making technology education and opportunities available to everyone, regardless of background.
  • Sustainable Progress: We prioritize technologies that address environmental challenges and promote sustainable development.

Through our conference, workshops, and community initiatives, we're building a global network of technologists dedicated to using their skills for positive impact. TechCon 2024 continues this tradition with a focus on AI ethics, climate tech, digital accessibility, and the future of work.
