Our History

TechCon was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate technology professionals who wanted to create a space where innovators could share ideas and collaborate. What started as a small local meetup has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences in the world.

Our first conference in 2015 brought together 200 technology enthusiasts.

Over the years, TechCon has expanded to include participants from over 50 countries and has featured groundbreaking presentations on artificial intelligence, blockchain, cybersecurity, and other emerging technologies.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation and collaboration in the technology industry. We believe that by bringing together diverse minds, we can solve complex problems and drive progress that benefits everyone.

Collaborative workshops are a key part of our mission to foster innovation.

Our core values include:

  • Promoting knowledge sharing across disciplines
  • Supporting diversity and inclusion in tech
  • Encouraging ethical technology development
  • Building bridges between academia and industry

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential minds in technology. Here are a few notable speakers from previous years:

AI Research Pioneer at NeuroTech Labs. Dr. Chen's groundbreaking work in neural networks has revolutionized how machines process complex information. Her 2021 keynote on ethical AI frameworks set new industry standards.

Chief Scientist at QuantumLeap Inc. Johnson's research has pushed the boundaries of quantum computing applications. His 2022 demonstration of quantum encryption showcased technology that could redefine digital security worldwide.

Dr. Aisha Patel

CEO of BioSynth Technologies. Dr. Patel's work at the intersection of biology and technology has led to breakthroughs in personalized medicine. Her 2020 presentation highlighted how AI can accelerate medical innovation.
